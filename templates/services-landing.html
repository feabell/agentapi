FAQ

Why Do I Have To Do This?
To make management of our communications services easier, your EvE account will be tied to your Slack and Discord accounts. Having a character in a WiNGSPAN alliance corporation, with a subscribed EvE account is a requirement for access to WiNGSPAN communications services.
What Permissions Do You Need From My API?
We have a setup a preset access mask with the required permissions. The solo permission we require is the "Account Status" flag so that we can verify your EvE account is active. At least one character on the provided API must be a member of a WiNGSPAN corporation.
Why Can't My API Expire?
If your API expires, when we perform our checks to see if you are still eligible to be a member of the WiNGSPAN comms services we won't be able to get any data. So your Slack account will get deactivated and your Discord account will lose all roles.
What happens if my account lapses? Or I leave corp?
Your Slack account will be de-activated, but NOT deleted. Your Discord account will lose all roles. If you re-sub and/or rejoin a WiNGSPAN corporation your Slack account will be re-instated and you can re-auth on Discord with authbot to get your roles back..
I'm confused! Who can I speak to about this?
Feel free to drop an in-game mail or private message to your friendly neighbourhood Operations Director Dakodai
